Scheme Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a key central sector welfare scheme launched by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers' Welfare, Government of India. Introduced on February 24, 2019, the scheme aims to provide direct financial assistance to all landholding farmer families across the country to supplement their financial needs for purchasing agricultural inputs and supporting household needs.

Benefits & Financial Assistance

Eligible families receive a financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year, disbursed in three equal installments of ₹2,000 every four months. The funds are directly transferred to the Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts of the beneficiaries using the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system, ensuring full transparency.

Installment Period Amount Disbursal Method
1st Installment April – July ₹2,000 Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
2nd Installment August – November ₹2,000 Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
3rd Installment December – March ₹2,000 Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Eligibility Criteria

  • All landholding farmer families who own cultivable land in their names as per official state land records.
  • The family unit is defined as husband, wife, and minor children.
  • Farmers from both rural and urban areas are covered under the scheme.

Exclusion List

  • Institutional landholders.
  • Families where one or more members hold or held constitutional posts (e.g., Ministers, MPs, MLAs).
  • Retired or serving government employees (except Group D / Multi-Tasking Staff).
  • Superannuated or retired pensioners with a monthly pension of ₹10,000 or more.
  • Income tax payers in the last assessment year.
  • Professionals like Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, and Chartered Accountants.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card: Mandatory for identity and OTP verification.
  • Land Ownership Documents: Khatauni, Jamabandi, or copy of land registration showing cultivable land ownership.
  • Aadhaar-Linked Bank Passbook: For receiving the direct transfer.
  • Active Mobile Number: Required for registration and SMS status updates.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official PM-Kisan portal at pmkisan.gov.in.
  2. Click on the 'New Farmer Registration' link in the Farmers Corner section.
  3. Select 'Rural Farmer Registration' or 'Urban Farmer Registration' based on your location.
  4. Enter your Aadhaar number, mobile number, select your state, and fill in the captcha, then click 'Get OTP'.
  5. Enter the OTP received on your mobile and proceed to the registration form.
  6. Fill in your personal details (District, Block, Village, Name, Category) and land ownership details (Survey/Khata Number, Dag/Khasra Number, Land Area).
  7. Upload scanned copies of land documents, enter your bank details, and click 'Submit'.
  8. Alternatively, approach your local Common Service Centre (CSC) or village Patwari/Lekhpal.

Status Check

  1. Go to pmkisan.gov.in.
  2. Under 'Farmers Corner', click on 'Know Your Status'.
  3. Enter your Registration Number. If you don't know it, click 'Know your registration number' and verify using mobile or Aadhaar OTP.
  4. Enter the captcha code and click 'Get Data'. Your installment details, Aadhaar seeding status, and eligibility status will be displayed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is eKYC mandatory for PM Kisan beneficiaries?

Yes, eKYC is mandatory for all beneficiaries. You can complete it online using Aadhaar OTP on the portal or via biometrics at any CSC.

What should I do if my installment is not received?

Verify if your bank account is linked with Aadhaar and Active for DBT. You should also check the 'Beneficiary Status' page on the portal for any rejection reasons like incorrect land mapping or name mismatch.

Can multiple members of the same family claim benefits?

No. The scheme guidelines define a family as husband, wife, and minor children. Only one member of the family can receive the benefit on their land holding.
