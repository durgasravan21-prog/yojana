Scheme Overview

The Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) 2026 is a key developmental initiative launched by the Government of India. This scheme represents a major milestone designed specifically to address critical needs in the Financial Inclusion & Business sector. By providing a comprehensive support mechanism, the initiative aims to target eligible beneficiaries across all states and Union Territories, ensuring that the benefits of developmental growth reach the grass-roots level. Under the supervision of the respective nodal Ministry, the scheme is backed by a substantial budgetary allocation, reflecting the government's commitment to social welfare, economic empowerment, and national development. Through transparent implementation channels such as Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT), Aadhaar-linked verification, and digital application tracking, the program minimizes intermediaries and maximizes efficiency, making it one of the most trusted welfare initiatives in the country today.

Benefits & Financial Assistance

A credit-linked subsidy scheme for setting up micro-enterprises. Offers capital subsidies of 15% to 35% on project costs up to ₹50 Lakh for manufacturing units and ₹20 Lakh for service units.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Any individual above 18 years of age with at least Class 8 education (for projects above ₹10 Lakh in manufacturing).
  • Self Help Groups, Co-operative societies, and charitable trusts.
  • Implemented by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) at the national level.

Exclusion List

  • Institutional landholders or corporate entities.
  • Families with retired or serving government employees (except Group D/MTS staff).
  • Income tax payers from the preceding financial assessment year.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card: Mandatory for biometric verification and identity mapping.
  • Proof of Address: Voter ID, utility bills, or domicile certificate.
  • Bank Account Passbook: Aadhaar-seeded bank account copy showing IFSC and account details.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official portal designated for the scheme at www.kviconline.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, select the 'New Registration' or 'Apply Online' option.
  3. Fill in basic details including Aadhaar card number, mobile number, and select your state/district.
  4. Verify your mobile number using the One-Time Password (OTP) received.
  5. Complete the comprehensive application form by entering personal details, bank information, and family declaration.
  6. Upload scanned copies of all required documents in the prescribed format (PDF/JPEG).
  7. Review the entered details carefully to avoid rejection, then click on the 'Submit' button.
  8. Save or print the generated Application Reference Number for tracking your status.

Status Check

  1. Go to the official portal at www.kviconline.gov.in.
  2. Click on the 'Know Your Status' or 'Beneficiary Status' tab on the homepage.
  3. Select your search parameter: Application Reference Number, Mobile Number, or Aadhaar Card Number.
  4. Enter the required number and fill in the security captcha code.
  5. Click on 'Get Data' or 'Check Status' to display your application approval stage and payment status.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the maximum subsidy under PMEGP?

The subsidy is 25% for general category and 35% for special categories (SC/ST/OBC/Women/Minorities) in rural areas, and 15% (general) / 25% (special) in urban areas.

What is the maximum project limit under PMEGP?

The maximum project cost eligible for subsidy is ₹50 Lakh for manufacturing sector units and ₹20 Lakh for service/business sector units.

What is the lock-in period for the subsidy?

The subsidy amount (margin money) is kept in a fixed deposit for 3 years at the financing bank and is adjusted against the loan account after successful verification.
