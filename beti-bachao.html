Scheme Overview

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on January 22, 2015. It is a joint initiative of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, and Ministry of Education. The scheme aims to address the declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and resolve issues related to women empowerment, survival, and education of girls.

Benefits & Financial Assistance

Rather than a direct cash transfer scheme, BBBP is an advocacy campaign combined with multi-sectoral action. It ensures survival and protection of the girl child, eliminates sex-selective abortion, promotes girl education (including building separate toilets in schools), and works closely with savings schemes like Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana.

Focus Area Action Taken Target Outcome
Prevention of Gender Bias Strict enforcement of PC-PNDT Act (anti-sex determination) Improve Child Sex Ratio at birth
Girl Child Survival Promote institutional deliveries and nutrition programs Reduce infant mortality rates in girls
Education & Safety Ensure girl-friendly school infrastructure and zero dropouts Universal secondary education for girls

Eligibility Criteria

  • All Indian families with a girl child, with campaigns focused primarily on districts with a low Child Sex Ratio.
  • All girl children studying in government or recognized private schools.

Exclusion List

  • None. The scheme campaigns and policy supports are applicable universally across all Indian districts.

Documents Required

  • No direct registration needed: This is a public policy campaign. However, to benefit from linked schemes (like Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana), standard documents like Birth Certificate and Guardian Aadhaar are needed.

How to Apply

  1. As BBBP is a national social campaign, families do not apply to it directly.
  2. To participate or secure benefits, enroll your girl child in nearby government schools where fee waivers, free uniforms, and cycles are provided.
  3. Open a Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana account at the nearest post office.

Status Check

  1. Information about local camp events, scholarships, and initiatives under BBBP can be verified via your local Gram Panchayat or District Magistrate's office.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Does Beti Bachao Beti Padhao offer direct cash payouts?

No. Beware of fraud. The government does not distribute direct cash to individuals under BBBP. The funds are utilized for school construction, public awareness, girl child healthcare, and district-level welfare infrastructure.

What is the primary goal of the BBBP scheme?

The primary goal is to prevent gender-biased sex-selective elimination, ensure survival and protection of the girl child, and facilitate her education.
