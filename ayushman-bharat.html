Scheme Overview

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is the flagship national health insurance scheme of the Government of India, launched on September 23, 2018, by PM Narendra Modi. Touted as the world's largest government-funded healthcare program, it aims to provide free healthcare access to over 50 crore citizens representing the bottom 40% of India's population.

Benefits & Financial Assistance

The scheme provides a health cover of ₹5,00,000 (₹5 Lakh) per family per year. This coverage is completely cashless and paperless at all empanelled public and private hospitals across the country. It covers all pre-existing conditions from day one of the policy.

Feature Coverage Details
Coverage Amount ₹5 Lakh per family per year (floating basis)
Pre-existing Diseases Covered from Day 1
Hospitals covered All empanelled public & private hospitals pan-India
Pre & Post Hospitalization 3 days pre-hospitalization & 15 days post-hospitalization costs (including medicines & diagnostics)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Families identified under the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data.
  • Rural families living in kucha houses, female-headed households, landless households, or households with disabled members.
  • Urban families engaged in occupational categories like ragpickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers, plumbers, etc.
  • In recent updates, all senior citizens aged 70 and above, regardless of income status, are eligible to receive a separate cover of ₹5 lakh under PM-JAY.

Exclusion List

  • Families owning a motorized vehicle, fishing boat, or 3-4 wheeler agricultural equipment.
  • Government employees.
  • Families earning more than ₹10,000 per month.
  • Tax-paying households.
  • Families living in permanent (pucca) houses with modern amenities.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card or Ration Card: For verifying family members and identity.
  • PM-JAY Letter / SECC Letter: Official letter sent by the government containing family ID (HHID).
  • Active Mobile Number: For linking and mobile authentication.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official website pmjay.gov.in and click on 'Am I Eligible'.
  2. Enter your mobile number and captcha, then enter the OTP received.
  3. Search for your family using name, ration card number, mobile number, or HHID number.
  4. If your name is listed, visit the nearest empanelled hospital or Common Service Centre (CSC).
  5. Approach the 'Ayushman Mitra' desk at the hospital or the operator at the CSC.
  6. Provide your Aadhaar card and Ration Card for biometric verification.
  7. Upon successful verification, the operator will issue your customized 'Ayushman Card' (e-card) which can be used to avail cashless hospital treatments.

Status Check

  1. Visit beneficiary.nha.gov.in.
  2. Log in using your mobile number and verify via OTP.
  3. Select your State, Scheme (PM-JAY), District, and Search By parameters (Aadhaar/Ration Card/Name).
  4. View the verification status of all family members. You can download active Ayushman cards directly from this portal.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there a limit on family size under PM-JAY?

No, there is no restriction on family size, age, or gender under PM-JAY. All members listed in the official ration card or SECC data can avail the benefits.

Do I need to pay any premium for the Ayushman Card?

No, the scheme is completely free. No premium or card charge is levied on eligible beneficiaries at government desks.

Can I get treatment in another state?

Yes. PM-JAY offers national portability, meaning a beneficiary can get free treatment at any empanelled hospital across any state in India.
