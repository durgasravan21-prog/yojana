Government Scheme Contact & Helpdesk Assistance Guide

At Yojana Guide, we assist Indian citizens by providing clear, fact-checked explanations of government scheme eligibility rules, document requirements, and step-by-step application procedures. While our editorial team manages this information portal, official scheme applications and financial disbursals are executed directly by respective Central and State Government Ministries.

If you have specific questions about our website content, notice outdated guidelines, or wish to suggest new welfare schemes for editorial coverage, please fill out the contact form above or email us at durgasravan21@gmail.com. For direct official grievances regarding your scheme application status or pending bank payments, please consult the official helpdesk channels outlined below.

PM-Kisan Helpdesk

For PM Kisan Samman Nidhi installment queries, contact the official toll-free helpline at 155261 or 011-24300606, or visit pmkisan.gov.in.

Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY

For cashless hospital treatment or Ayushman Card verification, call the 24x7 toll-free helpline 14555 or visit pmjay.gov.in.

PM Awas Yojana (PMAY)

For housing subsidy or application tracking, call toll-free 1800-11-6163 (Gramin) or 1800-11-3377 (Urban), or visit pmayg.nic.in / pmay-urban.gov.in.

Frequently Asked Questions About Contacting Us

What is the typical response time for contact form queries?

Our editorial team reviews incoming messages daily. You can expect a response to your email within 24 to 48 working hours (Monday through Friday).

Can Yojana Guide process official scheme applications?

No. Yojana Guide is an independent educational portal. Official applications must be submitted directly through designated government portals (such as pmkisan.gov.in, pmjay.gov.in, or myscheme.gov.in) or at authorized Common Service Centres (CSCs).

How do I report inaccurate or outdated scheme information?

We welcome feedback! Please send us an email at durgasravan21@gmail.com with the scheme name, URL, and specific details that need updating. Our fact-checking team will verify and update the article promptly.

Are there any fees charged for accessing Yojana Guide guides?

No. All scheme guides, eligibility calculators, document checklists, and application walkthroughs on Yojana Guide are 100% free for all citizens. We do not collect payments or registration fees of any kind.

User Feedback & Portal Improvement Policy

We continuously update our content based on user suggestions and official gazette notifications. If you are an official from a state agriculture, housing, health, or welfare department and wish to share updated scheme guidelines or official helpline updates, please write to our lead reviewer at durgasravan21@gmail.com.

Our editorial team conducts weekly verification reviews to ensure all public scheme summaries, eligibility criteria, document checklists, and application links remain 100% accurate, up-to-date, and compliant with official government releases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Yojana Guide and government schemes

❓ Is Yojana Guide a government website?

No, Yojana Guide is an independent information portal. We compile and present government scheme information from official sources to make it easily accessible to citizens. We are not affiliated with any government department or ministry.

❓ Is the information on this website accurate?

We strive to maintain the highest level of accuracy by sourcing information from official government websites, gazette notifications, and press releases. However, government policies can change frequently, so we always recommend verifying details on the official scheme website before applying.

❓ Can I apply for government schemes through this website?

No, you cannot directly apply for schemes through our website. We provide detailed information about how to apply, including links to official application portals where available. All applications must be submitted through the official government channels.

❓ How often is the content updated?

Our team monitors government announcements daily and updates articles as soon as any changes to schemes are announced. Major updates such as new installment releases, changes in eligibility criteria, or interest rate revisions are published promptly.

❓ Can I suggest a scheme to be covered on this website?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our readers. If there is a government scheme that you would like us to cover in detail, please send us a message through the contact form above and we will prioritize it in our content calendar.

❓ Is the information available in Hindi?

Currently, our content is available in English. We are working on adding Hindi language support in the near future to make our content accessible to a wider audience. Stay tuned for updates on our multi-language support.
