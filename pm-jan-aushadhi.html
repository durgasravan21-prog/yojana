Scheme Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) 2026 is a key developmental initiative launched by the Government of India. This scheme represents a major milestone designed specifically to address critical needs in the Health & Medical sector. By providing a comprehensive support mechanism, the initiative aims to target eligible beneficiaries across all states and Union Territories, ensuring that the benefits of developmental growth reach the grass-roots level. Under the supervision of the respective nodal Ministry, the scheme is backed by a substantial budgetary allocation, reflecting the government's commitment to social welfare, economic empowerment, and national development. Through transparent implementation channels such as Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT), Aadhaar-linked verification, and digital application tracking, the program minimizes intermediaries and maximizes efficiency, making it one of the most trusted welfare initiatives in the country today.

Benefits & Financial Assistance

Provides quality generic medicines, surgical items, and medical consumables at prices 50% to 90% cheaper than equivalent branded medicines in the market.

Eligibility Criteria

  • All citizens of India can buy medicines from Jan Aushadhi Kendras without restrictions.
  • Doctors are encouraged to prescribe generic names instead of branded formulations.
  • Individuals/NGOs can apply to open a Jan Aushadhi Kendra and receive up to ₹5 lakh government incentives.

Exclusion List

  • Institutional landholders or corporate entities.
  • Families with retired or serving government employees (except Group D/MTS staff).
  • Income tax payers from the preceding financial assessment year.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card: Mandatory for biometric verification and identity mapping.
  • Proof of Address: Voter ID, utility bills, or domicile certificate.
  • Bank Account Passbook: Aadhaar-seeded bank account copy showing IFSC and account details.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official portal designated for the scheme at janaushadhi.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, select the 'New Registration' or 'Apply Online' option.
  3. Fill in basic details including Aadhaar card number, mobile number, and select your state/district.
  4. Verify your mobile number using the One-Time Password (OTP) received.
  5. Complete the comprehensive application form by entering personal details, bank information, and family declaration.
  6. Upload scanned copies of all required documents in the prescribed format (PDF/JPEG).
  7. Review the entered details carefully to avoid rejection, then click on the 'Submit' button.
  8. Save or print the generated Application Reference Number for tracking your status.

Status Check

  1. Go to the official portal at janaushadhi.gov.in.
  2. Click on the 'Know Your Status' or 'Beneficiary Status' tab on the homepage.
  3. Select your search parameter: Application Reference Number, Mobile Number, or Aadhaar Card Number.
  4. Enter the required number and fill in the security captcha code.
  5. Click on 'Get Data' or 'Check Status' to display your application approval stage and payment status.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Are generic medicines as safe as branded ones?

Yes. PMBJP generic medicines are procured only from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers and are tested at NABL accredited labs to ensure therapeutic efficacy.

Who manages the PMBJP scheme?

The scheme is managed by the Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI) under the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers.

How can I find a Jan Aushadhi Kendra near me?

You can find local stores using the 'Jan Aushadhi Sugam' mobile application or by visiting the official PMBJP portal store locator.
