Our Mission

At Yojana Guide, we believe that every citizen of India deserves easy access to information about government welfare schemes and programs. The Government of India runs hundreds of schemes aimed at improving the lives of farmers, women, senior citizens, students, small business owners, and economically weaker sections of society. However, many eligible beneficiaries miss out on these benefits simply because they are unaware of the schemes or find the application process confusing.

Our mission is to bridge this information gap. We are a dedicated team of researchers, writers, and policy enthusiasts who curate, verify, and present government scheme information in a clear, easy-to-understand format. We cover everything from eligibility criteria and required documents to the step-by-step application process, ensuring that you have all the information you need to benefit from these welfare programs.

Founded in 2024, Yojana Guide has grown to become one of India's most trusted sources for Sarkari Yojana information. We cover central government schemes, state government schemes, and various welfare programs across sectors including agriculture, healthcare, housing, education, financial inclusion, and women and child development.

What We Cover

Our comprehensive coverage spans multiple sectors and types of government programs. We provide detailed, well-researched articles on each scheme to help citizens understand and access the benefits they are entitled to.

  • Agriculture & Farming: Schemes like PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, PM Fasal Bima Yojana, Kisan Credit Card, and irrigation subsidy programs that support India's farming community with financial assistance and crop protection.
  • Health & Medical: Programs including Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY), Janani Suraksha Yojana, and other health insurance and medical assistance schemes providing healthcare coverage to millions.
  • Housing & Shelter: Housing programs such as PM Awas Yojana (both Urban and Gramin), providing affordable housing and home loan subsidies to help every Indian family own a pucca house.
  • Education & Skill Development: Scholarship programs, skill development initiatives, and educational assistance schemes including PM Vidyalaxmi, National Scholarship Portal schemes, and Skill India programs.
  • Women & Child Development: Empowerment programs like Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana, Beti Bachao Beti Padhao, Ujjwala Yojana, and various maternity benefit and nutrition programs focused on women and children.
  • Financial Inclusion & Business: Schemes such as PM Mudra Yojana, Stand Up India, PM Jan Dhan Yojana, Atal Pension Yojana, and other financial inclusion programs promoting entrepreneurship and banking access.

Why Trust Yojana Guide?

We understand the importance of accuracy when it comes to government scheme information. Wrong or outdated information can lead to wasted time and missed opportunities. That is why we follow a rigorous editorial process to ensure every piece of content we publish is reliable and up-to-date.

  • Verified Information: All our content is sourced from official government websites, press releases, gazette notifications, and verified government documents. We cross-reference multiple official sources before publishing.
  • Regular Updates: Government schemes are frequently updated with new rules, increased benefits, or changed eligibility criteria. Our team monitors these changes and updates our articles promptly so you always have the latest information.
  • Simple Language: We translate complex government jargon and legal language into simple, easy-to-understand content. Our step-by-step guides make it easy for anyone to apply for schemes, regardless of their educational background.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Each article covers all aspects of a scheme — from overview and benefits to eligibility, required documents, application process, and frequently asked questions.
  • Free Access: All information on Yojana Guide is completely free. We believe access to government scheme information should not be behind any paywall or require any subscription.

Our Values

We are guided by core values that shape everything we do:

  • Accuracy: We prioritize correctness above all else. Every fact is verified before publication.
  • Accessibility: Information should be available to everyone in a format they can understand.
  • Timeliness: We strive to publish updates as soon as changes are announced by the government.
  • Transparency: We clearly state our sources and inform readers when information may have changed.
  • Impact: Our goal is to help as many eligible citizens as possible access government benefits.
